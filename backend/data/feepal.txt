### 1.1. Project Summary

FeePal is a dedicated mobile-based financial management application designed to address significant administrative inefficiencies and a pervasive lack of transparency within the Pakistani school system concerning fee collection and payment tracking. Traditional manual, paper-based record-keeping often results in delayed payments, administrative errors, communication gaps, and a lack of real-time financial oversight.

FeePal offers a centralized digital ecosystem that automates fee reminders and streamlines payment tracking for educational institutions, ensuring financial accountability and administrative ease. It aims to replace traditional paper-based processes with a digital solution that facilitates transparent financial communication between school administrations and parents.

### 1.2. Core Innovation

A core innovation of FeePal is its **Automated Reminder and Voucher Generation System**. This system is designed to:
*   Promptly inform parents of upcoming deadlines through scheduled notification alerts.
*   Reduce the burden of manual follow-ups for school administration.
*   Allow parents to generate digital payment slips (vouchers) directly through the app, facilitating flexible financial planning and payment installments.
*   Ensure a documented history for every transaction.

### 1.3. Dual-Portal Interface

The application features a robust, role-oriented access model with distinct portals:
*   **Admin Dashboard:** For school administrators to monitor overall financial health, track pending dues, manage student fee records, and gain real-time insight into financial Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and total collections.
*   **Parent Portal:** For parents to securely access detailed fee breakdowns, view payment histories, generate digital vouchers, manage installment plans, and receive timely notifications, thereby fostering a more transparent and convenient payment process.

### 1.4. Development Methodology

FeePal is being developed using the **Agile Scrum methodology**. This iterative-incremental approach, chosen for its flexibility and adaptability to evolving administrative requirements, ensures:
*   Iterative refinement of key features (e.g., notification engine, voucher generation).
*   Continuous feedback integration from stakeholders (school administrators, parents).
*   Early identification and resolution of technical issues (e.g., database synchronization, payment logic).
*   A balance between functional application development and evolving documentation (Sprints, User Stories).
*   The platform is robust, thoroughly tested, and grounded in specific user needs.

## 3. Technical Details and Architecture

### 3.1. Technology Stack

*   **Operating System:** Android (mobile application).
*   **Frontend Framework:** Flutter (Dart) for building a responsive and cross-platform UI.
*   **Backend Services:** Google Firebase (Spark/Free Tier for development).
    *   **Authentication:** Firebase Authentication for secure user login, registration, and role-based access control.
    *   **Database:** Cloud Firestore (NoSQL) for real-time data synchronization, storing student fee records, transaction logs, and notification data.
    *   **Storage:** Firebase Storage for storing physical files, specifically images of paid bank vouchers uploaded by parents.
    *   **Messaging:** Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) for push notifications (fee reminders, overdue alerts, payment confirmations).
*   **Local Storage:** Shared Preferences (on-device storage) for session management (login tokens), user preferences (language, theme), and cached metadata (student name, school logo).
*   **Additional Services (from Appendix A):** Email Service, Cloudinary Service, OCR Service (Google ML Kit), OTP Service, SMS Service, Voucher Generation Service.
*   **Development Tools:** Visual Studio Code (Primary IDE), Android Studio (for SDK management and emulator support), Flutter SDK, Git & GitHub (for version control and collaboration).
*   **Project Language:** Dart.

### 3.2. System Architecture: Three-Tier Layered Architecture

The FeePal system employs a Three-Tier Layered Architecture to ensure modularity, scalability, and ease of maintenance by separating user experience from core logic and data management.

#### 3.2.1. Presentation Layer (UI Modules)

This layer handles all user interactions and visual elements using the Flutter Framework.
*   **Authentication Module:** Secure login interface for Admins and Parents, including input validation, role-based access, and "Forgot Password" flow.
*   **Admin Dashboard Module:** Primary control center for school staff, offering navigation for student records, fee structures, and collection status (centralized grid view for KPIs).
*   **Parent Dashboard Module:** User-friendly interface for guardians to track financial status, view digital vouchers, check payment history, and initiate installment requests.
*   **Voucher & Payment Interface:** Displays digital fee vouchers and allows interaction with payment tracking features, including uploading bank deposit slips and real-time payment status overlays.
*   **Notification & Alert View:** Manages the visual delivery of push notifications and SMS alerts for critical reminders.
*   **Reporting & Analytics View:** Displays aggregated data for administrators (monthly collection reports, defaulters) and progress bars for parents.

#### 3.2.2. Service Layer (Business Logic)

This layer contains the core intelligence of the application, processing data between the Presentation Layer and the Data Persistence Layer.
*   **Fee Management & Installment Logic:** Calculates outstanding amounts, manages fee splitting into installments, validates payment plans, and updates balances in real-time.
*   **Notification & Alert Scheduler:** Monitors due dates, triggers push notifications/SMS alerts based on priority (e.g., escalating from "Reminder" to "Priority Warning" for overdue payments).
*   **Voucher Generation Engine:** Aggregates student data, class-specific fee structures, and current balances to generate unique, downloadable PDF/image-based vouchers.
*   **Verification Manager:** Processes uploaded paid voucher slips, marks records as "Pending Verification," and updates payment status system-wide upon admin approval.
*   **Auth Service:** Encapsulates Firebase Authentication for secure user sessions, credential validation, password resets, and strict enforcement of role-based access.
*   **Reporting Service:** Gathers raw data from the database and performs aggregations (e.g., total monthly revenue, defaulter lists) for the Admin Dashboard.

#### 3.2.3. Data Persistence Layer (Backend & Local)

This layer handles long-term storage, retrieval, and integrity of all system data, ensuring synchronization and accessibility.
*   **Cloud Firestore (NoSQL Database):**
    *   **Users Collection:** Stores unique profiles for Admins and Parents, including authentication UIDs, contact information, and assigned roles.
    *   **Student Records:** Detailed information for each student (class, section, roll numbers).
    *   **Fee Ledger & Transactions:** Core financial repository storing fee structures, monthly voucher statuses, payment history, and records of uploaded bank slips.
    *   **Notification Logs:** Tracks the history of sent SMS and Push Notifications.
*   **Firebase Storage:** Dedicated cloud storage for images of Paid Bank Vouchers.
*   **Local SharedPreferences (On-Device Storage):**
    *   **Session Management:** Stores login tokens for persistent user sign-in.
    *   **User Preferences:** Remembers language settings (English/Urdu) and theme (Light/Dark mode).
    *   **Cached Metadata:** Stores basic profile info (student name, school logo) for instant UI loading, even offline.

## 4. Key Features and Business Logic

### 4.1. User Management and Authentication

*   **User Registration:** Admins register themselves by providing name, email, and password. Parent accounts are provisioned by Admins (phone number and auto-generated password).
*   **User Login:** Secure login using Firebase Authentication, with role-based redirection to Admin or Parent dashboard.
*   **Email Verification:** Ensures account authenticity upon Admin registration (link expires in 24 hours).
*   **Forgot Password:** Secure reset link sent to registered email (single-use, time-bound tokens).
*   **Profile Management:** Admins update personal/school details; Parents update personal details.
*   **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):** Strict enforcement of privileges (e.g., Admins modify financial tools, Parents access only their children's data).

### 4.2. Fee Management Workflow

*   **Student Fee Record Creation:** Admins create student records and assign specific fee structures (tuition, charges) linked to a unique Student ID.
*   **Fee Template Creation & Rollover:** Admins define foundational fee templates for classes/groups with base tuition, transport fees, and default due dates. An Automated Rollover Engine duplicates these templates for new months, applying any Admin updates to future vouchers.
*   **Generate Fee Voucher (Parent):** Parents can dynamically generate and download digital fee vouchers (PDF/image) for selected months or installments. Vouchers include unique transaction ID and a clear breakdown of charges.
*   **Installment Management:** Parents can create and manage installment plans for large fee amounts, subject to institutional approval. The system then divides the total fee into scheduled installments and tracks each independently.
*   **Payment Tracking:** The system tracks payment activities and updates statuses (Pending, Paid, Overdue). Status automatically becomes "Overdue" if the due date passes without verified proof.
*   **View Fee Reports (Admin):** Admins view uploaded and generated vouchers, and institutional KPIs (total collections, outstanding debt).
*   **View Payment History (Both):** Historical records of all payments accessible to both Admin and Parent roles (role-based visibility).

### 4.3. Automated Reminder and Notification System

*   **Scheduled Reminders:** Automated push notifications, SMS, and/or email alerts sent to parents 3-5 days before the fee due date.
*   **Overdue Alerts:** Automated alerts generated if a payment is missed after the deadline.
*   **Payment Confirmations:** Real-time notifications sent to parents immediately after an Admin verifies a payment.
*   **Notification Archive:** All alerts are stored within the app for tracking purposes.
*   **Notification Cooldown Logic:** Prevents spamming by enforcing a cooldown period (e.g., 7 days) between reminders for a specific parent/student.

### 4.4. Voucher Upload & Verification Process

**Business Logic Flow: "Fee Payment & Verification"**

1.  **Start:** Admin initiates "Generate Voucher" for a student/class.
2.  **Fee Calculation:** System retrieves Fee Structure and outstanding arrears.
    *   *Decision:* Is the student on an Installment Plan?
        *   **Yes:** Calculate specific installment amount due.
        *   **No:** Generate full monthly fee amount.
3.  **Notification Trigger:** System generates digital PDF/Image voucher and sends Push Notification/SMS to Parent.
4.  **Payment Loop (Parent Side):**
    *   Parent views voucher in Parent Portal.
    *   Parent pays fee via bank.
    *   **Action (Proof Upload):** Parent takes photo/screenshot of proof of payment and uploads it via the "Upload Paid Voucher" module.
5.  **Verification Phase (Admin/System Side):**
    *   Uploaded image is stored in Firebase Storage.
    *   Record status changes to "Pending Verification."
    *   **Local OCR Verification Module (Google ML Kit):** The image is processed locally on the parent's device to extract keywords (bank identifiers, transaction IDs, dates, amounts). This ensures privacy and speed.
    *   *Decision:* Is the proof valid (ML Kit successfully identified required stamps/transaction ID)?
        *   **No:** System rejects proof, triggers "Re-upload Required" notification to parent. If too blurry for OCR, it's flagged as "Pending Verification" for manual review.
        *   **Yes:** System (or Admin via Manual Review if OCR fails) accepts voucher.
6.  **Data Synchronization:**
    *   **Update Ledger:** System marks the month as "Paid" in Cloud Firestore.
    *   **Balance Update:** Arrears are cleared, and total outstanding balance is recalculated.
    *   **Confirmation:** "Payment Successful" message sent to Parent Portal.
7.  **End:** Return to Dashboard (both portals).

### 4.5. Admin Specific Functionality

*   **Admin Dashboard:** Real-time KPIs (Total Students, Fees Collected, Pending Payments, Overdue accounts), Quick Actions (Add Student, Create Fee).
*   **Student Management Screen:** Live global search (by Name/Roll Number), Bulk & Single Onboarding (Import from Excel, Add New Student form), Student Record Cards (name, class, roll no., active status), linked to detailed profiles.
*   **Fee Management Screen:** Temporal Billing Tabs (Current/Next Month), Class-Based Search, Fee Record Actions (Edit, Delete fee structure), Floating Action Button for "Create Fee."
*   **Reminder & Notification Screen:** Triple-tab navigation (Standard, Priority, History alerts), Notification Cooldown Logic (countdown before re-sending), Parent-Student Mapping, Real-Time Data Refresh.
*   **Admin Profile Page:** Institutional Branding (school logo upload), Editable Field Management (School Name, Address), Core Admin Data, Bank Account Details gateway.
*   **Manual Review and Approvals:** Admins can manually view and override status for receipts flagged as "Pending Verification" if OCR fails.

### 4.6. Parent Specific Functionality

*   **Parent Dashboard:** Overview of child's financial status, current month's outstanding fee, rolled-over arrears, history of verified payments.
*   **View Fee Records:** Detailed academic dues and breakdown of individual charges (tuition, transport).
*   **Check Payment History:** Chronological list of all previous payments, uploaded proofs, and their verification status.
*   **Voucher Generation Screen:** Student billing summary (Name, Class, Monthly Fee, Additional charges, Total Due), Voucher Selection & Deadline, Installment Request Tool, Export & Download (PDF).
*   **Parent Profile Screen:** Centralized overview of account information, linked student profiles, verification of contact details.
*   **Parent Notification Preferences:** Manage notification settings.
*   **Contact School:** In-app option to contact school.

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements

### 5.1. Usability (USA)

*   **USA-1 (Parent Interface):** User-friendly, intuitive icons, clear financial terminology, legible font sizes for easy mobile navigation.
*   **USA-2 (Admin Interface):** Facilitates quick input of student fee records and efficient updating of payment statuses for multiple students.
*   **USA-3 (Error Messages):** Straightforward (e.g., "Invalid Voucher Format") and suggestive of rectification steps.
*   **USA-4 (Status Indicators):** Clear visual distinction between "Paid," "Pending," and "Overdue" in Fee Record Management view.

### 5.2. Performance (PER)

*   **PER-1 (Login):** User login (Admin or Parent) within 5 seconds under typical network conditions.
*   **PER-2 (Dashboard/History Load):** Student fee dashboard and payment history logs load in under 3 seconds.
*   **PER-3 (Voucher Generation):** Generation and download of digital fee vouchers processed quickly.
*   **PER-4 (Notifications):** Automated reminder engine triggers push notifications precisely on the scheduled date (e.g., 5 days before deadline).

### 5.3. Reliability (REL)

*   **REL-1 (Data Storage/Sync):** Consistently stores and syncs all financial transactions and payment proofs in Cloud Firestore.
*   **REL-2 (Calculations):** Tracking of fee installments and remaining balances correctly calculates and reflects changes upon payment verification.

### 5.4. Security (SEC)

*   **SEC-1 (Passwords):** Firebase Authentication securely manages and encrypts Admin passwords.
*   **SEC-2 (RBAC):** Strictly enforces Role-Based Access Control to block unauthorized access to school-wide financial history or other students' private fee information.
*   **SEC-3 (Data Encryption):** Sensitive data encrypted at rest and during transit using industry-standard protocols.
*   **SEC-4 (Local OCR Privacy):** Local OCR system ensures sensitive financial documents are processed exclusively on the user's local hardware, uploading only the finalized image payload to secure Google Cloud Storage buckets once authorized.

## 6. Testing & Evaluation

### 6.1. Testing Methodologies

*   **Unit Testing (UT):** Verified separate financial computation modules and isolated backend utilities (e.g., installment split calculations, localized format translations).
*   **Functional Requirements Testing (FT):** Focused on user-facing features (e.g., digital voucher generation, verification proof uploads) against user expectations.
*   **Integration Testing (IT):** Confirmed smooth collaboration among various client-side interface blocks, concentrating on real-time transactional synchronization with Firebase Cloud Firestore services.

### 6.2. Key Test Cases Highlighted

*   **UT-01 Multi-Month Arrears Verification:** Confirmed accurate summation of multiple months of outstanding arrears (e.g., Rs. 5,000 for April + Rs. 5,000 for May = Rs. 10,000 total).
*   **UT-02 Installment Allocation Splits:** Verified division of a lump sum into equal installments (e.g., Rs. 15,000 into three Rs. 5,000 payments).
*   **UT-03 Local Storage Auth Session Persistence:** Verified local Shared Preferences retain authentication hashes across app restarts.
*   **UT-04 Scheduled Reminders Cooldown Window:** Confirmed notification cooldown flags prevent immediate re-sending of alerts within 7 days.
*   **UT-06 Unique Voucher ID Generator:** Verified proper string generation for unique voucher IDs.
*   **UT-07 Late Fee Penalty Addition Check:** Verified compound fine application rules when voucher date exceeds due window.
*   **IT-02 New Digital Invoice Generation to Live Parent Dashboard Delivery Sync:** Verified admin-side invoice creation reflects immediately on parent dashboards.
*   **IT-07 Verification Evidence File Streaming to Firebase Cloud Storage Buckets:** Verified secure upload of payment proof images to cloud storage.
*   **IT-08 Local Interrupted Connectivity Queue Offline Caching to Active Restoration Transmission:** Verified transaction uploads during offline periods save locally and sync to cloud upon connectivity restoration.
*   **IT-09 Verification Fail Trigger Cycles to Parent Alert In-App Deliveries:** Verified admin rejection of proof dispatches accurate correction notices to parent.
*   **IT-10 Secure Role-Based Access Routing Interception Validations:** Verified security blocks parent accounts from accessing admin modules.
*   **IT-17 Firestore Database Connection Failures to Interface Status Banners:** Verified app displays offline warnings upon database connection failure.
*   **IT-19 Dynamic Token Expiry Sign-Out Enforcements:** Verified expired authentication tokens clear access and reroute to login.
*   **IT-20 Account Block Toggles to Real-Time Session Termination Handshakes:** Verified admin blocking an account instantly terminates active sessions.

## 7. Limitations and Challenges

### 7.1. Current System Limitations (Identified during development/testing)

*   **Reliance on Manual Fee Updates:** Admin must currently input student fee data and update payment statuses manually.
*   **Limited Payment Integration:** Does not provide direct in-app online payment processing; parents must pay through external channels.
*   **Internet Dependency:** Core functionalities (login, data synchronization, voucher retrieval, notification delivery) require an active internet connection.
*   **Scalability Concerns:** Database operations and synchronization may require further optimization for larger educational institutions.
*   **The Scope of Financial Support:** The system tracks financial balance but does not provide direct emotional support or professional counseling for families facing extreme debt.

### 7.2. Potential Challenges (from Feasibility Study)

*   **Digital Access and Adoption:** Potential barrier for some parents due to lack of mobile devices or internet access.
*   **Integration with Existing Systems:** Significant effort required to connect with established, legacy school information systems for full-scale commercial implementation.
*   **User Training and Implementation:** School staff may need training to transition from manual ledgers to digital management.
*   **Child and Parent Data Privacy:** Requires robust security protocols and compliance with data privacy laws, necessitating continuous vigilance in Firebase security rules.
*   **Technical Maintenance and Updates:** Ongoing maintenance, bug corrections, and OS compatibility updates are beyond the FYP scope.
*   **Scope Creep:** Managed through a strictly prioritized Product Backlog due to the iterative nature of Agile.
*   **Data Integrity:** Ensuring manual updates accurately reflect in the database to prevent "false overdue" alerts.
*   **User Adoption:** Encouraging traditional school administrations to transition to a fully digital tracking system.
